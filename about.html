The problem we set out to solve

Engineering teams are productive in silos.
That's a bug, not a feature.

Students and junior devs spend more time hunting for tools than actually building. Every session starts from scratch — no shared context, no continuity, no intelligence on what the team already knows. Existing tools are either built for solo use or enterprise scale. Nothing in between.

Our solution

One workspace. AI at the centre.
Built for how teams actually work.

AI that understands engineering

Nova is tuned for circuit theory, algorithms, signal processing, and systems design — not generic Q&A. Ask it something actually hard.

Collaboration built in, not bolted on

Team planner, shared sessions, and a unified workspace mean everyone is on the same page from day one — not after three Slack threads.

Intelligence on your own data

Nova Insights turns your activity into actionable advice — not just charts. Know what your team has mastered and what still needs work.

Share any session — one link

Every Nova conversation can be shared with your team instantly. One link, read-only view, no sign-up required. Real-time knowledge sharing without the friction.

Nova plans which pages to fetch, browses them in real time via the server, and returns cited answers. Ask about today's news, latest docs, or anything beyond its training data.
