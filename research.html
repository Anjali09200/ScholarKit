Attention Is All You Need: Revisiting Transformer Architectures for Long-Context Reasoning

This paper presents a comprehensive re-evaluation of the original Transformer architecture with modifications targeting long-context reasoning tasks. Through novel positional encoding strategies and sparse attention mechanisms, the authors demonstrate significant improvements on benchmarks including MMLU, HotpotQA, and SCROLLS, achieving state-of-the-art results with 40% less compute compared to existing baselines.
